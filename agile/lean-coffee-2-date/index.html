Lean Coffee #2

Come and join us for another evening of lively discussions about Scrum, agile and whatever else you fancy.

We'll be using the Lean Coffee format which is a structured, but agenda-less, meeting that ensures discussions are relevant and productive; by having the topics democratically generated by the participants.

We will be in the upstairs function room of the The Ram in Widcombe which is a 5 min walk from Bath Spa Station.

A handful of on-street parking spaces are available outside the pub, otherwise the Southgate car park is only a short walk away.
