Exercises in Lean-Agile transformation

As described in Mike's recent blog post Lean-Agile transformation as Lean-Agile process we'll look at least one of the 'Discover' or 'Exploration' elements of the five part transformation process: 

Discovery
Identifying strategic goals, obstacles, and outcomes

Exploration
Debriefing your Agendashift values-based delivery assessment, agreeing scope and approach

Mapping
Building your transformation plan

Elaboration
Generating, framing, and developing actions

Operation
Organising for continuous transformation

Don't expect a long talk. Do expect a hands-on experience :-) Definitely some Clean Language. Time permitting some Cynefin also.

As it happens, the full workshop takes place locally the following day: Agendashift facilitator day, Bristol

Mike is the founder of Agendashift, author of the book Kanban from the Inside, consultant, coach, and trainer. In recent months he has been the interim delivery manager for two UK government digital "exemplar" projects and consultant to public and private sector organisations at home and abroad. Prior to his consulting career, he was global development manager and Executive Director at a top tier investment bank, and IT Director for an energy risk management startup.
