Agile Speed Dating

A crazy idea from the organising committee, to have short focused conversations about any subjects you like.  It's a mix of agile networking and knowledge exchange, with the usual opportunity for a natter after proceedings!

The concept is a mix of open space and speed dating.  We always get smaller groups in summer, this format takes advantage of that allowing you to get to know more members, and to talk through issues that interest you in depth.

We’ll agree the format on the night, but here’s some background to get you thinking:
