Brain Science tools for teams, coaches and trainers with Andy Hiles

A note about the location: this meeting is hosted by Civica Digital, which is the new name for regular hosts IPL. So the location is the same - Eveleigh House, Grove Street, Bath - but the name has changed!

Based on the highly popular Training from the BACK of the Room! book and course created by Sharon Bowman, this workshop demonstrates how to apply Brain Science principles to keep people engaged in any session you lead. 

This session is jam-packed with practical tips and strategies you can use immediately in your own workshops, coaching and presentations. 

About our speaker: 

Andy Hiles is a experienced Agile practitioner, event speaker and Agile trainer based in the south west of the UK. 

Working for Radtac across the training and consulting teams, Andy is driving Training from the Back of the Room! techniques in all of the training and coaching engagements.
