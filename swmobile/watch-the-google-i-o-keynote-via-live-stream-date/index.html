Watch the Google I/O Keynote via live stream

It's that time of year where we can kick back and watch the Google I/O Keynote via live stream from the comfort of Hargreaves Lansdown's conference space complete with food and refreshments. 

Watch with your fellow SWmobile members and discuss the new announcements as they happen! Bonus points if you can spot any SWmobile members who are over in San Francisco in person. 

If you haven't heard of Google I/O it's an annual developer conference held by Google in San Francisco, California. I/O showcases technical in-depth sessions focused on building web, mobile, and enterprise applications with Google and open sources such as Android, Chrome and Chrome OS, APIs, Google Web Toolkit, App Engine, and more.

As with previous events at Hargreaves Lansdown you'll have RSVP (so any guests will have to register as member) and bring photo id.
