Client-side Operation

For our next meetup we'll be discussing all things client-side.

Many of our past meetups have been supplier-focused. That is, we've looked at how we can make better apps or deliver a better service to our clients/employers.

This time we'll be flipping things around and looking at how to get the best from people when you're the client/employer.

We're proud to announce that this event will be sponsored by Elance who'll be providing a bar tab at the Colston Yard for after the event.

Agenda:

19:00 Arrive

19:10 Welcome and introduction

19:15 James Barlow from Elance - The continuum of engagement:  how to hire the right person for the right job and, how to work out what the right job is in the first place

19:45 Abby Frear - Secrets from an Ex-Recruiter

20:00-20:15 Wrap-up and head to the Colston Yard for networking and drinks
