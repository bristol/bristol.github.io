April talk night

This month we have three speakers, with the topics loosely based on data storage and push notifications.

The meetup starts at 6:15pm, where pizza and beer will be provided courtesy of Hargreaves Lansdown. Talks will begin at 7pm, and we will be off to the pub by 8:30-9pm.

Introduction to Couchbase Mobile - James Nocentini

Couchbase Mobile is a native document database for mobile and embedded devices, which makes it easier to build offline-first apps thanks to its automatic handling of two-way sync with your central database. James works as a developer evangelist for the platform, and is going to provide an introduction to the Couchbase platform.

Working with CloudKit - Dan Leivers

Dan provides a quick overview of CloudKit, the Backend-as-a-Service from Apple.

From Zero to Push in 10 minutes - Jake Chandrasakera

Featuring Parse and Google Glass - Jake shows us how he built a presentation app controlled by Google Glass. Very cool!

Afterwards we'll be heading to the pub, with beers and nibbles provided by our sponsors Couchbase and Hargreaves Lansdown.

Please arrive early if possible as due to the secure nature of the venue, we are required to officially sign in which means a PHOTO ID is required!
