February Meetup: Lightning Talks ⚡️

Tuesday, 21st February, 6:30pm

February's meetup will be another of our lightning talks sessions – 10 minute talks on a range of topics!

• James Frost: Mobile at Automattic

• Scott Alexander-Bown: Android Things (A Brief Intro) 

• Vincente Santacoma: Challenging the MacBook orthodoxy: Does mobile development require mobility?

• Dan Leivers: Unit Testing

• Liam Richardson: Why Apps Are Bad and the Web is Good

• Sam Davies: Rendering Images and PDFs in iOS 10 with UIGraphicsRenderer 

The wonderful cxpartners will be hosting us again, and you can expect the usual refreshments, talks, and a trip to a nearby pub afterwards.

If you're interested in giving a lightning talk, please get in touch with us! You can contact us through Meetup.com, Slack, or by replying to this email.

If you're not used to giving talks, lightning talks can be a great way to get started: they're short, we're a friendly group, and you can talk about anything that you're passionate about that's related to mobile development.

Thanks!
