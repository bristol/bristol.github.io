Talk 1: Pillarisation: modular architecture using pillars at Hotels.com 

Richard Rauser, freelance iOS Developer @ Just Eat

Maciej Matyjas, Director, Technology @ Hotels.com

Wojciech Rutkowski, iOS Engineer @ Hotels.com

Structuring your iOS app as a set of independently runnable and testable app "pillars" can produce serious benefits, like increased modularity, clearer separation of concerns, and the ability to effectively organise teams around product areas. Come learn how Hotels.com made this happen with Richard Rauser (iOS Developer @ Just Eat via 101 Ways), Maciej Matyjas (Directory of Technology @ Hotels.com), Wojciech Rutkowski (iOS engineer @ Hotels.com).

By Daniele Pietrobelli
In essence I will show a tutorial implementing and using some of the coding standards defined by Ray Wenderlich for Swift and show how you can create a file with these and integrate it in your project.
