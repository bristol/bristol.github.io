Whats New in Mobile

Welcome to Junes SWmobile meetup  "What's new in mobile" hosted at Just Eat. As usual please only RSVP if you are certain you can make it and update accordingly. Thanks

Thanks to Just Eat for providing the venue and catering for the evening and to our group sponsors for providing funds to cover the 1st round at the pub afterwards.

Talk 1: Firebase: 2017 update and the habits of highly successful developers

We'll be kicking off with a talk from Laurence Moroney who's a Firebase Developer Advocate at Google taking us through what's new in Firebase for 2017.

Talk 2: What's new in iOS

SWmobile's very own James (@frosty) Frost will be giving us an update on what's new, interesting, fun and impactful for all things iOS and WWDC.


Talk 3: Update from Google IO 2017

Dave.T, Dave.J and Scott were lucky enough to attend Google IO this year and will be sharing their key takeaways,  behind the scenes info and which talks out of the 100 or so recorded that are worth a watch.
