Talk night #6: Going Freelance

Freelancing/contracting in the mobile industry is thriving, but often making the first steps on to the freelancing ladder can be daunting. I hoped with our great pool of freelancers and founders we could share some of the lessons learnt, quick tips and tools. Please email me suggestions for short talks and volunteers to share their experiences will be essential.

Confirmed speakers :

- Tim Wicksteed - fellow SWmobile member

- Nicolas Alpi - From the Freelancers Breakfast meetup group

- Richard Spence - fellow SWmobile member

Followed by a panel style discussion

Ideas for topics of discussion:

- Getting work
- Building a portfolio website
- Finances and tax
- Best bits and the worst bits
- Tips
