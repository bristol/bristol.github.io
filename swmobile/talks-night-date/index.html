Talks Night

The SWmobile talk nights conclude for 2015 with three top-notch talks, once again hosted by the kind folks at Just EAT.

Talk #1: I'm a little IoT pot: Ben Howes, CEO and founder of zoetrope.io

Ben talks about the defining features of IoT and how to distinguish it from the gimmicks. Followed by a section on IoT for mobile developers.

Talk #2: SWmobile review: Scott Alexander-Bown, founder of SWmobile meetup group

Scott runs through some of the highlights of the year and outlines plans for next year's goodies at SWmobile.

Talk #3: Creating and Publishing Your Own Awesome Open Source Android Libraries: Danielle Emma Vass, freelance mobile developer

Would you like to start contributing towards open source projects but don’t know where to start? Or have your own project idea but don’t even know where to begin? This talk aims to get you started with Awesome Material, an open source library which helps developers incorporate Material Design, Bootstrap, and Font Awesome into their apps.

Afterwards we'll be heading to the pub, where as usual the first drink will be on us, thanks to our sponsors.
