Lightning Talk Night

June's meetup is going to be a little different - we're going to be doing lighting talks! If you're unfamiliar with the concept, this means each talk will be only 10 minutes long. It should be a really fun evening, with lots of speakers and a wide range of topics - something for everyone! Here's our list of speakers:

• Paul Hudson: UIStackView For Fun and Profit

• Karl Nosworthy: 10 Top Software Engineering Concepts and Processes

• James Frost: Cocoapods vs Carthage: iOS Dependency Management

• James-Lee: I <3 Swift

• Sam Davies: Functional Reactive Programming (FRP FTW!)

• Chris Phin: Personality and Playfulness in App Design
