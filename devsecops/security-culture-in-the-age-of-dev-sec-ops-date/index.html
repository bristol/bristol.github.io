It's debate time!

Cloud native processes and tooling have accelerated the speed at which organisations can bring new products and services to market.

Organisational structures are morphing away from rigid departmental silos to facilitate x-functional teams that are empowered to move fast (and sometimes break things).

Yet often security teams remain siloed away from what is happening across the rest of the business. Why?

And in this brave new world of cloud, kubernetes and serverless technologies... who owns security? Do we need to create a shared responsibility model for internal teams?

Perhaps the biggest question of all - how do we inculcate a security culture when the business has a negative perception of security?

All to be (hotly) debated! We'll have 2 teams going head to head with a series of questions where the audience gets to choose who they think is right. It's going to be loud, it's going to be fun, and we'll all walk away a little wiser than before.
