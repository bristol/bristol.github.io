Clojure Study Group (Spec)

This week we will be trying Clojure Spec - The spec library specifies the structure of data, validates or destructures it, and can generate data based on the spec. (https://clojure.org/about/spec)

• What we'll do - This study group is for people who are want to learn or improve their knowledge of Clojure (https://clojure.org/) and ClojureScript (https://clojurescript.org/)

No prior experience with Clojure, Lisp, functional programming, or coding is required.

All levels of experience is welcome, but for the initial meetings we will be focusing on the basics.

• What to bring
A laptop and an open mind.

It would be helpful if you have Leiningen (https://leiningen.org/) installed and an IDE with a Clojure plugin, though not essential.

• Important to know

Limited Short stay parking is available.

Doors open at 6.30pm. We will be help anyone who needs help with their set up.

We will start at 7pm
