PHPub Quiz 2015

For the end of 2015 it's a welcome return to our now annual tech-themed Pub Quiz.

The questions will be techy, but not too serious, geeky, or necessarily, even PHP-orientated. There'll be a bar tab along with some bar snacks and we're in the process of putting a selection of prizes together.

We're thinking teams of 4, if you don't have enough we'll pair you up, and we'll be hosting at the Bristol Ram on Park Street.
