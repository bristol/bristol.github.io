End of Year Social 2017

Twas a few weeks before Christmas, when all through Bristol
Not a creature was stirring, not even an ElePHPant.

In a PHPSW tradition, we're finishing up the year with a social, rather than a normal evening of talks.

This year we'll be hosting it upstairs at the Famous Royal Navy Volunteer. We'll be providing a couple of drinks for everyone and a small buffet.

As well as plenty of time for chatting and socialising, we'll be organising a few activities (possibly including the return of JeoPHPardy) and giving away plenty of prizes too.

As always, a big thanks to our meetup sponsors Ents24, Brightpearl, Meanbee, Deep Blue Sky & Equiniti without whom we wouldn't be able to put on events the likes of which we have this year. 

We'd also like to thank @phptraininguk who are kindly buying the first round of drinks, and Laracon Online for providing us with two tickets to include as raffle prizes.
