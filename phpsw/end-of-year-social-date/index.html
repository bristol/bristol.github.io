End of Year Social

This month we're skipping the talks and teaming up with Bristol JS (https://www.meetup.com/BristolJS/) to have an end of year social.

Although mainly a good opportunity for networking, we'll be looking to put on a quiz or something similar for a few giveaways too.

Join us to see out the year, discuss your experiences and start planning for 2020!

SR2 – Socially Responsible Recruitment (https://www.sr2rec.co.uk/) in Bristol are kindly helping us host this evening, allowing us to have a larger space so more people can attend. Thanks to them!
