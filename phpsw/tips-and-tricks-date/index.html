Tips and Tricks

In June we're hosting a night of Tips and Tricks with lightening talks from many speakers, including.... 

- Tips on PHP tools, Windows and Git
Doug Fitzmaurice, Developer at ENTS 24

Tips and tricks for tools: Amiss, King, XDebug, ConEmu, Kitty and lots of Git related goodies  (Power Prompt, rebase -i, share alias and many more).

- Zend Apigility
Jan Franciszek Kowalski, Backend PHP Web Developer

Zend Apigility it the world's easiest way to create high quality APIs. Find out how in this Lightening talk. 

- PHPStorm 
Nathan McBride, Certified Magento Developer at Meanbee

Tips on what you can do with PHPStorm

- Vim with all the things 
Luke Sturgess-Durden, Operations and Development Lead at Deep Blue Sky

Pick up some Vim tips from Luke

- Amazon SQS
Lee Stone, Web Team Lead at Gradwell

Get an introduction as to why you should consider Amazon SQS as a queuing solution.

- CI tips
Tom Adam,  Developer at Instantiate

A brief dip into CI tools.

- Tmux
Daniel Leech, Open source PHP developer involved in the Symfony CMF and is the developer of PHPBench

An intro to Tmux.
