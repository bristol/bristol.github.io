Bio:  Matteo Meucci has more than 15 years of specialising in Application Security and collaborates from 2002 with the OWASP project: he founded the OWASP-Italy Chapter in 2005 and leads the OWASP Testing Guide from 2006. Matteo is invited as speaker at many events all around the world talking about Web Application Security. Matteo has undergraduate degrees in Computer Science Engineering from the University of Bologna.

Matteo Meucci is the CEO and a cofounder of Minded Security from 2007, where he is responsible for strategic direction and business development for the Company. Prior to founding Minded Security, Matteo had several consultancy experiences from BT Global Services, INS, Business-e and CryptoNet.
