Meeting #6: accu2013 Conference Retrospective

The ACCU Conference made a big move this year, from its traditional home in Oxford to a new location in Bristol.

If you went to this year's conference, come along and tell us about the sessions you attended and discussions you enjoyed.

And if you couldn't make it, come and hear about what went on this year.

This is an informal evening where members can catch up on everything that went on at this year's conference over a few drinks at the Knights Templar, a stone's throw from Temple Meads.
