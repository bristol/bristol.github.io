SRE Bristol - the first event

Welcome to the first Bristol Site Reliability Engineering meetup!

We'll be hosting the event at OVO Energy's Rivergate office, based near to Bristol Temple Meads (beer and pizza will be provided!)

**Schedule:

19:00 Arrive, grab a beer, pizza and chat

19:30-20:00: Tom Cammann (Production Engineering Manager, OVO Energy) will talk about OVO's approach to SRE, their SRE journey to date, some of the challenges/hurdles that were overcome along the way

20:00-20:30: Tim Berry, SRE Team Lead at Claranet will give a talk on 'Infrastructure as Code with Terraform: Codifying your infrastructure'; bring the benefits of configuration management to your platform layer, with better quality control, predictable deployments and even shiny pipelines. This talk will give a brief introduction to Terraform, a vendor-neutral declarative tool that defines your infrastructure as code.

20:30-21:00 - more beer, pizza and a chance to network
