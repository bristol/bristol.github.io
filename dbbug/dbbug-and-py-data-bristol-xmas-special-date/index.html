DBBUG & PyData Bristol Xmas Special

We are delighted to announce a joint Christmas social event with fellow Bristol-based Python meetup, the PyData Bristol group.

** CHANGE OF VENUE**
We will be meeting at Runway East Bristol this time, for those coming by train it is approximately 12 minutes' walk from Bristol Temple Meads.
Address: 1 Victoria St, Bristol BS1 6AA

Massive thank you to Elucidata for kindly sponsoring the venue hire.

You can expect several 5 minute Christmas themed lightning talks from both PyData and DBBUG groups' members, as well as extended breaks and networking time plus community announcements. There will be a staffed bar on site, and free mince pies!

If you would like to give a Christmas lightning talk on the evening then please fill in your relevant details on the spreadsheet below. We are always looking for new speakers and lightning talks.

==============

Doors open at 18:00, talks kick off at 18:30 sharp, beers available from the bar before the talks start and during the intervals. There will be a designated pub to head to afterwards.
