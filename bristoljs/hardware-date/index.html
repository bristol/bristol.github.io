Espruino - JavaScript for Hardware

A look at some of the challenges of developing with hardware, and how using JavaScript can let you create power-efficient software quickly and easily - with a few demos along the way!

Chris McKirgan - Hacking with hardware

Hardware such as the Arduino Leonardo allows us to emulate various devices, opening up a great deal of yet unseen creative opportunities with not always honest intentions. 

Sam Machin - CHIP, RaspberryPi and the $10 Echo

Sam will be talking about the various 'cheap' computers that have emerged and some of the pros/cons of a couple of them. He'll also talk briefly about his $10 Echo project turning a CHIP into an Amazon Echo.
