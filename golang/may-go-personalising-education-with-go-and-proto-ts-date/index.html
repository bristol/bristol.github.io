üó£ Max Huxtable (@matthewhuxtable) - Personalising Education with Go

Matt is a Senior Site Reliability Engineer at Sparx, an Exeter-based education technology company which works with secondary schools to improve outcomes in Mathematics. The Sparx platform supports thousands of students and their parents & teachers on a weekly basis in setting, marking and analysing work targeted specifically to the needs of each learner. Over the last two years, the Sparx tech stack underwent a major rewrite from Python to Go, which coincided with a migration onto Google Cloud Platform and adoption of a slew of new technologies: Kubernetes, GRPC, and many others.

This talk explores the role technology can play in personalising education, the power of the Go ecosystem in supporting these efforts, and some of the lessons learnt along the way. Matt founded the SRE team at Sparx, where he principally focusses on ensuring the platform and its engineering team has the tools, technology and processes it needs to bring Sparx to the masses. When he's not hacking on Go or convincing Kubernetes who's really at the helm, you can find him experimenting with molecular gastronomy, being fascinated about aviation, or dreaming about one day being a qualified skydiver.
