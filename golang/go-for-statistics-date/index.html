Go for statistics

This month we welcome Eleanor (@unoxymoronic) from the nearby Cardiff Go Meetup with a talk she gave earlier this year at Gophercon UK about Go's role in publishing data at the Office for National Statistics!

Thanks to NatWest for kindly hosting us this month.

* Please bring a form of ID with you, e.g. a driving license or passport! *

Here is the plan with (infeasibly precise) timings.

18:15 - Arrive at the RBS Group Building reception for food & chat (BS2 OPT)

18:30 - Welcome/Start

18:35 - "go-ns - Go's role in publishing data at the Office for National Statistics" - Eleanor Deal

ONS needed to take obtuse datasets and publish them in a searchable way - so citizens can start to make sense of statistics the affect them. Using Go we've rebuilt how data gets prepared, a pipeline for custom queries and extended our go-ns library - learning more about the practical sides of Go.
