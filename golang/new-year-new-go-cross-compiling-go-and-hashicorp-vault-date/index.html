New Year, New Go - Cross-compiling Go & Hashicorp Vault

Hello Bristol Gophers 👋!

Join us again for another round of great talks 🗣, food 🍕 and drinks 🍻. This time at Brightpearl's offices 🏢! As always, it's going to be great!

🗣 George Oikonomou - Cross compiling and the compiler evolution

George is a Principal Software Engineer at Actual Experience, where he mostly works on building cross-platform client services.

🗣 Ben King - Hashicorp's Vault and Go Plugins

Hashicorp's Vault is a powerful, yet intimidating tool! After a brief introduction into Vault and some tips on how to get started, we will be diving into Vault's most complex component: Plugins. Ben currently works at Tumelo, where he helps with the Golang-based backend.

🍺 As always, afterwards we will be heading to the pub for a drink.
