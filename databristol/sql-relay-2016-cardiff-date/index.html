SQL Relay 2016: Cardiff

Lets join our friends across the bridge in Cardiff for the all day SQL Relay event this year.  Multiple tracks, great speakers and free training! 

"October 4th 2016, SQL Relay is back in Cardiff for the fifth time. SQL Relay is a conference focused on the Microsoft Data Platform. SQL Relay Cardiff is set during working hours, in the city centre, and free to make it easier for everyone who wants to attend to join us. We're even sorting transport for the folks who might want to attend but are stuck due to the Severn Tunnel closure. A nice, friendly conference we hope to see you and your colleagues there!"
