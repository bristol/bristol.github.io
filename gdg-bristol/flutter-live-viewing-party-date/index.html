Flutter Live! Viewing Party

We're having a party!

On 4th December Google are hosting Flutter Live! - An event where they will be sharing a variety of news and updates about Flutter.

The session on 4th starts early, so we hosting a viewing party the day after on Wed 5th Dec where you can come and eat pizza, have some drinks, and watch the re-run of the livestream with us. You will also have the opportunity to network with your peers and ask us any questions that you may have.

After the stream, we will do a code lab which we encourage you to follow along. You'll be able to see just how easy it is to put together a fully functional Android/iOS app with a single codebase written in Dart.

Here's the agenda:

6:00 pm - Attendee arrival, drinks and networking
6:30 pm - Flutter Live! keynote speeches
8:00 pm - Break/drinks/pizza
8:15 pm - Flutter Code Lab
9:00 Close/pub

We really hope you will be able to join us. There will be some great highlights from the keynote!
