Mr. Data's ML, AI and AIY Workshop

Join us in welcoming our new robot overlords as we discuss all things big data, machine learning and artificial intelligence. We'll explore Google's range of off-the-shelf automated machine learning services and APIs and get hands-on with a Google AIY Vision Kit!

See https://aiyprojects.withgoogle.com/ for more information.
