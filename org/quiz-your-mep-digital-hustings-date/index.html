Quiz your MEP: Digital Hustings

Join ORG Bristol to hear from the MEP candidates for the South West and quiz them on their views on digital rights.

In the run-up to the European elections (22nd May), Open Rights Group are holding MEP hustings across the country, to ask candidates to pledge to protect digital rights.

The pledge will be tied into the WePromise 10 point charter.

Candidates speaking

• Georgina Butler - Conservative 

• Audaye Elesedy - Green

• Julia Reid - UKIP

• Jay Risbridger - Liberal Democrat

• Hadleigh Roberts - Labour
