ORG Bristol 'Hustings'­ - Quiz your future MP on digital rights

Bristol North West residents (and those interested in nearby areas) are invited to attend this Hustings, a free to attend public debate where you can ask parliamentary candidates in your constituency questions about issues that matter to you.

Not sure if you live in Bristol North West? Find out here: <a>https://yournextmp.com</a>;

Invited Candidates Include: Charlotte Leslie MP (Conservative) - definitely attending, Justin Quinell (Green), Darren Jones (Lab), Clare Campion-Smith (Liberal Democrat), Michael Frost (UKIP).

This event is supported by ORG Bristol as part of the Greater Bristol Alliance who've organised it. The Greater Bristol Alliance is a coalition of local campaign groups. There will be prepared questions on topics including: TTIP, austerity and surveillance.

For more info on the Greater Bristol Alliance and the event: https://greaterbristolalliance.wordpress.com/2015/03/25/public-meeting-9th-april-how-is-austerity-the-answer-and-is-ttip-the-last-straw/

We will confirm timings of the event nearer to the date.

This event is free and open to the public. Please arrive at the Greenway Community Centre from 7pm onwards.

See you there!

Lydia 
ORG Bristol Organiser
