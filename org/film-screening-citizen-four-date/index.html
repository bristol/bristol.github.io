Film screening: CitizenFour

Hi all,

If you missed the CitizenFour film premiere at the Watershed last month, the Cube microplex are doing two further screenings:
o Mon 24th Nov @ 8 p.m
o Tue 25th Nov @ 8 p.m

The Tuesday screening will also include a Q&A with Milena Popova from ORG.

CitizenFour is a thrilling documentary set in Hong Kong, showing the tense meetings between Laura Poitras, Glenn Greenwald and Edward Snowden, when they discuss the NSA leaks, plus Snowden's reactions to the leaks as they hit the media.

For more info see here:
http://www.cubecinema.com/programme/event/citizenfour,7556

Please note that advanced tickets have sold out for both screenings. However there will be twenty tickets on the door for £5 each on a first-come-first-served basis.
