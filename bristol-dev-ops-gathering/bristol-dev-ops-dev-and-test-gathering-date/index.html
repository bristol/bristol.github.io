Bristol DevOps, Dev & Test Gathering

Speakers TBC.

The DevOps, Dev & Test Gathering brings together experts, and those interested in IT, in an informal social environment. During the evening, there are talks from leading industry experts, free welcome drinks and the opportunity to get to know colleagues across the broad spectrum of IT.

We aim to encourage cross-disciplinary wisdom through the sharing of experiences and ideas from all corners of the SDLC.

Accessibility: the room is accessible (ramp and lift available) however the venue does not have a disabled bathroom.
