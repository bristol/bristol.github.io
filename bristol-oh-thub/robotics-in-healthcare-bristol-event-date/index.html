Bristol OHT Hub is having a relaunch in 2018!

We're starting with a blockbuster of an event featuring four stars of the Robotics in Healthcare scene talking about their work and careers. Plus, you'll have the chance to have your say on what you'd like to see at future One Healthtech events in Bristol, and to meet some new people over free drinks. What's not to like!

AGENDA

19:00 - Registration & Networking

20:00 - Welcome 20:05 - 3 x 10 minute talks on Robotics in Healthcare by:
- Samantha Payne - COO & Founder of Open Bionics, a multi-award-winning Bristol-based robotics company developing affordable and stylish bionic hands.
- Sabine Hauert - Assistant Professor in Robotics at University of Bristol. Sabine's profoundly multi-disciplinary research focuses on designing swarms that work in large numbers (>1000), and at small scales
- Antonia Tzemanaki - Lecturer/Researcher at Bristol Robotics Laboratory, University of the West of England, with a focus on surgical robotics.

20:35 - Healthtech careers: a woman's perspective (1 x 5 minute talk and Speaker Panel Q&A_
Rav Bumbra - Founder of Structur3dpeople, a tech recruitment company on a social mission to increase diversity and inspire a new generation of talent within the tech industry.

21:00 - Networking & Refreshments
