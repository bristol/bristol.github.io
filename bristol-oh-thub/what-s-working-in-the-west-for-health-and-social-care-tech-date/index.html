What’s Working in the West for Health & Social Care Tech?

Join One HealthTech in Bristol for an evening of fresh, thought-provoking discussion focused on the transformation of health and social care in the West of England.

PLEASE RESERVE YOUR PLACE VIA EVENTBRITE: https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/whats-working-in-the-west-for-health-social-care-tech-tickets-50956783110

We’ll be looking at some of the success stories from our region, celebrating key digital and tech projects, and learning from the highs and lows. We’ll also consider how we put people at the centre of health & social care to ensure that all our transformational energy is heading in the right direction.

Join us for drinks & nibbles to share your stories of excellence, and tell us what you think other regions can learn from the West of England.

ONE HEALTHTECH is a network that supports and promotes women and other underrepresented groups in health innovation, in order to build a health system that works for everyone.
