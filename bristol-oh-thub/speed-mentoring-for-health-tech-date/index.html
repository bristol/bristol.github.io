Speed Mentoring for HealthTech

Gearing up for a career boost in the New Year? Or perhaps it's time to pass on some of your hard-earned wisdom and gain a new perspective in the process. Mentoring is a simple but powerful way to support underrepresented groups in HealthTech, pay it forward, and generally develop your career.

** As well as joining on Meetup, please get your ticket via Eventbrite https://bit.ly/2CqR956. If you only see a small number of attendees on Meetup it's because it's not our main platform **

Join One HealthTech in Bristol for an interactive evening as we find out how mentoring makes a difference to both individuals and organisations. We'll also launch our own mentor matching activity.

Discover how you can be your own cheerleader and how to approach a mentor. Learn the dos and don’ts around forming a mentoring relationship. We’ll also quiz a bunch of people who’ve been a mentor or mentee. And if that's not enough, you can also take part in a speed-mentoring session!

Many thanks to our refreshment sponsor West of England AHSN and venue sponsor Runway East Bristol.
