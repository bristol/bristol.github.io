AR/VR in Health and Social Care

Find out what VR/AR has to offer child and adult mental health, and how it can connect people and technology in new ways.
-----------------------
As well as joining on Meetup, please register for your ticket at https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/arvr-in-health-and-social-care-tickets-44839723821
-------------------------
Following our lively & well-received relaunch event in February, the Bristol OHT Hub now invites you to join them on a journey into the worlds of AR & VR, expertly guided by:
* Ayleen Driver from Evoke Education
* Andrew Jackson from ProReal
* Heather McDonald Tait from Ultrahaptics

Ayleen will introduce us to Moe the Monkey, a computer-generated hologram that helps children communicate and could help them open up about sensitive information.

Andrew will show how ProReal plans to tackle mental health problems with avatar-based therapy, for which they’ve been awarded an SBRI grant.

Heather will present the groundbreaking technology behind Ultrahaptics that creates the sensation of touch in mid-air, and explain how they plan to use this in healthcare.
