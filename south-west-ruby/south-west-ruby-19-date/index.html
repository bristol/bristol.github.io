About the speaker

Julie is a software engineer originally from the States but on assignment in London. She works at Stitch Fix, a ML-assisted personal styling company as a full-stack engineer (RoR, Golang, React). Always building side-projects either just for fun or to be the next big thing, Julie enjoys the ease of using Ruby to get MVP's up and running. In her free time, she enjoys reading, country music, American sports, and finding dogs to pet.
