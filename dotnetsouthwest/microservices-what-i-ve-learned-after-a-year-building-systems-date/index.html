Microservices: What I’ve learned after a year building systems

This talk goes into my experience over the last couple of years building systems based on microservics architecture.

Whilst I'll mention the technology used this talk won't be about the technology more about general principles and the lessons learnt during these projects.

I'll cover how we built the system, deploying the code & most importantly how to support the system.

About Nathan Gloyn

Nathan is a passionate developer and firm believer in agile principles. In his day time job he is contractor helping clients deliver working software.

Nathan has successfully boot strapped scrum &amp; agile teams in various companies that he has worked at and has given presentations on technology and agile at a number of events.
