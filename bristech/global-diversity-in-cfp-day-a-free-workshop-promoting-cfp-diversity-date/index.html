Global CFP Diversity Day - A free half-day workshop promoting CFP diversity

As part of the Global Diversity CFP day (https://www.globaldiversitycfpday.com/) Bristech are proud to be hosting a half-day workshop on all things CFP, including how to choose a topic, write a decent CFP, an introduction to public speaking and pointers to further resources and information to help people with their CFP applications.

This is of relevance not only to prospective Bristech speakers, but anyone who wishes to submit more successful applications to CFPs for conferences and the like.

The event will be facilitated by public speaking practitioners and will also feature Bristech speakers past and present, as well as members of the selection panel for 2019 for their insights into how selections are made for the autumn conference.
